Our PayPal Services


For websites that only offer PayPal as a payment option,
we can now make the purchase for you.

Please review the following steps on making a purchase using PayPal.

Process


Please send the following information to our
email contact@optimum-lb.com for review:

  • 1. Link of website from which you wish to purchase your item(s).
  • 2. If a username and password are required to login, please provide them so that we may login to review your purchase request.
  • If you do not wish to provide your login details, simply create a fictitious login for the first time. If your order is approved, you'll be asked for the real login details.
  • 3. List all the required steps to complete the purchase.

Our Fees


Our current fee schedule is 5% on any payment requested,
with a minimum fee of $3 USD.

How to Pay


Currently we accept the following methods
(please contact us for further details):

  • 1. Western Union
  • 2. Pay at the bank (at any Byblos bank branch)
  • 3. Pay by hand (in Dekwaneh)
  • 4. We also accept (for small payment amounts) mobile dollars sent to an Alfa number we provide.
